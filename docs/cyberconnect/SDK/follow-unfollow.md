#### Follow

```ts
cyberConnect.follow(address, handle);
```

- `address` - Current signed in wallet address.
- `handle` - The target handle.

#### Unfollow

```ts
cyberConnect.unfollow(address, handle);
```

- `address` - Current signed in wallet address.
- `handle` - The target handle.
